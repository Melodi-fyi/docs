---
title: 'What is Melodi?'
---

**Melodi is the essential platform for AI teams aiming to enhance customer engagement.** 

It showcases how to effectively track product performance through the Melodi Analytics dashboard, which monitors key metrics like retention and user feedback. Melodi not only aids in tracking AI models but also supports data science teams with detailed reports and access to underlying data for content updates.

Melodi is great for:

* **Product Managers** who want to understand their customers and prioritize high-impact features.

* **Operations Managers** who want to report on the success and engagement of AI initiatives.

* **Data Scientists** who need quick access to the right datasets for few shot examples, fine-tuning, and custom testing.
